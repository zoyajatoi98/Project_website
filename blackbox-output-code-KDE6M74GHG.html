About the Project

The Grove at Great Neck is a premier residential development by Toll Brothers, offering modern condominiums in a prime location. With stunning architecture, high-end finishes, and convenient access to New York City, it's designed for upscale living. Key features include:

  • Spacious 1-3 bedroom units
  • Resort-style amenities (pool, fitness center, lounge)
  • Proximity to Long Island Rail Road and major highways
  • Sustainable design with energy-efficient systems

Location: 10 Bond St, Great Neck, NY 11021. View on Google Maps.
